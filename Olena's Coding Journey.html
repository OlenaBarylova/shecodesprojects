Olena's Coding Journey

Once upon a time, in the bustling city of Amsterdam, there lived a busy lady named Olena. She was a 41-year-old refugee from Ukraine, seeking a fresh start in her new home.

One day, Olena stumbled upon a flyer for She Codes Workshops, a series of coding workshops designed to empower women in tech. Intrigued by the idea of learning something new, Olena decided to attend the workshops.

With no prior experience in coding, Olena found herself at the very beginning of her coding journey. It was a challenging endeavor, but she knew that hard work and perseverance were key to her success.

Day by day, Olena immersed herself in studying this new field. She spent countless hours in front of her computer, tirelessly learning and practicing her coding skills.

Throughout her journey, Olena faced both triumphs and obstacles. There were moments of frustration and exhaustion, but she never lost sight of her goal.

Whenever Olena felt overwhelmed, she would take a break and enjoy a comforting cup of tea. It served as a reminder that she had a strong support system and the determination to continue her coding journey.

Slowly but surely, Olena's coding skills began to flourish. She started building her own projects, solving complex problems, and achieving milestones she once thought were impossible.

And so, Olena's touchy and lively story of perseverance, determination, and the pursuit of knowledge goes on. She knows that through coding, she can shape her own future and make a difference in the world.
